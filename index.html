SignalNest Studio

A web-based research tool for organizing public social content and consumer feedback into aggregated demand signals for U.S. market research.

View Demo Terms of Service Privacy Policy
Research use only

SignalNest Studio helps analyze public content and comments to understand consumer pain points, product discovery behavior, and social trend signals.

How It Works

Collect

Import public posts, comments, reviews, source URLs, and engagement signals from authorized sources.

Organize

Group public user language into pain points, recommendation requests, workarounds, and product-gap signals.

Analyze

Generate aggregated research outputs for internal market analysis and niche demand discovery.

Use of TikTok Data

When authorized TikTok developer capabilities are used, SignalNest Studio is intended to process public TikTok content and comments for aggregated research. It does not post content, modify user accounts, access private messages, or request non-public TikTok user data.
