Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SignalNest Studio handles information when used as a market research tool for analyzing public social content and consumer demand signals.

1. Information We Process

The service may process public social media content, public comments, public engagement metrics, source URLs, timestamps, and other publicly available metadata provided through authorized APIs or user-provided research inputs.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The service does not intentionally collect private messages, payment information, government identifiers, precise location data, passwords, or sensitive personal information. The service does not request access to non-public TikTok user content.

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used to generate aggregated research outputs, such as consumer pain-point clusters, demand signals, market trend summaries, and product discovery insights. The service is designed for aggregated market research rather than individual profiling.

4. Data Storage

Research data may be stored locally or in secure internal research files for analysis and auditability. Source URLs and public text may be retained to verify research results.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Research outputs may be shared internally or with authorized collaborators for market research and business evaluation purposes.

6. Third-Party Platforms

The service may rely on third-party APIs or data providers. Use of those services is subject to their own terms, privacy policies, and developer rules.

7. Data Requests

If you have questions about data handled by the service, contact us and include enough information for us to understand and review the request.
